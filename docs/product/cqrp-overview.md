# CQRP Product Overview

CQRP (COA Quant Research Platform) is a research-first system for evaluating the COA methodology before any live-capital use. Its objective is evidence: replayable market observations, deterministic decisions, paper execution, portfolio risk controls, and auditable analytics.

## Product boundaries

- **Research:** capture market data, replay sessions, compare strategy versions, and retain immutable evidence.
- **Decision support:** compute COA, validation, signals, risk decisions, scanners, and explainable research results.
- **Paper trading:** simulate execution and lifecycle events for validation.
- **Live execution:** remains separately gated by configuration, OMS safety controls, and human authorization.
- **Copilot:** currently offline and evidence-only; it cannot make or alter trading decisions.

## Success criteria before live-capital expansion

1. Repeatable positive expectancy across sufficient paper trades and regimes.
2. Acceptable drawdown and portfolio exposure under documented limits.
3. Reproducible experiment, replay, and analytics results.
4. Complete audit trail from snapshot through decision and outcome.
5. Human approval for every strategy promotion and execution-mode change.
