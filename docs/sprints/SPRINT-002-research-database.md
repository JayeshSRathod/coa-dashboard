# Sprint-002 — Research Database & Event Logging

## Goal

Create CQRP's versioned, append-only SQLite research store without changing the v1 dashboard, COA mathematics, or legacy trade ledger.

## Data captured

- strategy profiles and configuration DNA
- market/COA snapshots
- generated signals and their validation trail
- paper-trade lifecycle events
- system and data-quality events

## Design decisions

- UTC ISO-8601 timestamps are stored as text.
- IDs are UUIDs generated by the repository layer.
- structured explanations and raw COA/option-chain payloads are stored as JSON.
- research records are immutable after insertion; lifecycle state is represented by further events.
- migrations are ordered and recorded in `schema_migrations`.

## Out of scope

Live snapshot scheduling, UI integration, broker order placement, analytics queries, replay screens, and migration of the legacy `db/ledger.py` data.
