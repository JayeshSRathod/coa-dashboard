# CQRP 3.0 Release Notes

CQRP 3.0 establishes the enterprise-oriented platform layers introduced in Sprints 019–029: canonical market data and COA boundaries, decision/application APIs, workstation foundation, paper and OMS safety layers, enterprise risk, scanner intelligence, research strategy studio, and an offline evidence Copilot.

This is a research and paper-trading platform release. It does not authorize unattended or autonomous live trading.
